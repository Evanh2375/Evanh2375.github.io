About Eva

Teaching is about
genuine connection.

I'm Eva — a bilingual English teacher with over 20 years of experience helping professionals reach the next level of fluency. I specialise in Business English for IT, finance, legal, pharmaceutical, and marketing sectors, and hold a 100% success rate for IELTS preparation.

I also coach executives on high-stakes presentations and interviews, and mentor university students and doctoral candidates through academic writing and thesis completion. My approach is entirely student-centred: every course is designed around your industry, your goals, and your schedule.

I've worked with professionals from Deutsche Bank, Rolex, Repsol, Johnson & Johnson, Banco Santander, and many others across six continents.

Get in touch

Outside the classroom, I'm a mum of two spirited teenagers, a baker, music lover, and enthusiastic traveller. I lived in Barcelona, Tarragona, and London before settling on the island of Rhodes. I also teach Spanish and Greek — so if you're feeling adventurous, let's talk.
